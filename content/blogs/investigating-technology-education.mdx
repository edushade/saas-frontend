Technology in education is no longer optional—it's central to how we teach and learn. This post looks at the evidence linking technology use to student outcomes and what we can do to maximize impact.

## Key findings

- **Access matters**: Reliable devices and connectivity are baseline requirements.
- **Pedagogy first**: Tools amplify good teaching; they don't replace it.
- **Data with care**: Use analytics to support learners, not to surveil them.
