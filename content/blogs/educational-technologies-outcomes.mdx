Good design is pleasing to the eye; great design is invisible. When educational technology works well, learners focus on the content, not the tool.

## What we're building

We're investing in:

1. **Simpler interfaces** – Less clutter, clearer paths.
2. **Faster feedback** – Quizzes and checks that feel instant.
3. **Richer media** – Video, audio, and interactive elements where they add value.
