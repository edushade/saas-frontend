This Cookie Policy explains how Edushade ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our website and platform.

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how you use the service. We also use similar technologies such as local storage and pixels where relevant.

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

**Strictly necessary** – Required for the site to function (e.g., authentication, security, load balancing). These cannot be disabled without affecting core functionality.

**Functional** – Remember your choices and preferences (e.g., language, region, sidebar state) to improve your experience.

**Analytics and performance** – Help us understand how visitors use our platform (e.g., pages viewed, features used) so we can improve our services. We may use first-party or trusted third-party analytics providers.

**Marketing (if applicable)** – Used to deliver relevant content or measure the effectiveness of campaigns. Where we use such cookies, we will seek your consent where required by law.

## 3. How Long Do Cookies Last?

- **Session cookies** – Deleted when you close your browser.
- **Persistent cookies** – Remain for a set period (e.g., days or years) unless you delete them or they expire.

Specific retention periods depend on the purpose of the cookie.

## 4. Your Choices

**Browser settings** – Most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Doing so may affect site functionality and your experience.

**Opt-out tools** – For certain analytics or advertising cookies, we may provide or link to opt-out mechanisms.

**Consent** – In regions that require it, we will ask for your consent before placing non-essential cookies.

## 5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use (e.g., analytics, support widgets). Their use of data is governed by their own privacy and cookie policies. We encourage you to review those policies.

## 6. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or in law. The "Last updated" date below will be revised when we do. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance.
